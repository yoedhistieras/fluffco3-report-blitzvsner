Executive Summary

Account Overview

The New AI Scale Engine is showing real early promise - it is attracting higher-value buyers and generating stronger mid-funnel engagement than the Legacy Statics Engine on a per-visit basis. But it is not yet producing returns at the efficiency we need, because it is being asked to generate original concepts rather than evolve what is already proven. At the same time, the Legacy Statics Engine is carrying the account, with a handful of aging creatives that have been live too long now acting as the main drag on overall performance. The path forward is straightforward: fix the inputs going into the AI Scale Engine, retire the creatives that are past their peak, and the account has a clear route back above the 1.9x ROAS target.

✓ What's Performing

The hotel comfort and offer positioning is the strongest creative pattern we have. It works across both engines and consistently produces the best ROAS and CPA in the account. It should anchor the next brief cycle.

The AI Scale Engine is reaching better buyers. Average order value of $157 versus $146 from Legacy tells us the broader creative testing pool is finding higher-consideration customers. That is a valuable signal as we scale the engine.

AI Scale top performers all started as Legacy winners. Every top-performing AI Scale creative this period was an iteration of a proven Legacy asset, not a new concept. The iteration model is working when applied correctly.

⚡ The Core Challenge

Both engines are slightly below the 1.9x ROAS target, and it traces back to one root cause: a small group of Legacy creatives running since December are pulling the account average down. This is a portfolio maintenance issue, not a structural one.

The AI Scale Engine is being under-utilized. When AI assets are generated independently without a Legacy creative foundation, they consistently underperform. The engine has more potential than the current results show - it just needs the right brief process to unlock it.

The checkout experience on get.fluff.co is leaving revenue on the table. A consistent drop between Add to Cart and Checkout is visible across both engines. This is a site-side opportunity that no amount of creative investment can fix on its own.

→ Strategic Direction

Treat the AI Scale Engine as an evolution layer, not a replacement layer. Its job is to take what already converts in Legacy and build volume around it through visual iteration. That is where it delivers value.

Retire aging Legacy creatives proactively and redirect that budget toward the winners we have identified. The efficiency recovery does not require new creative investment - it requires stopping spend on assets that have run their course.

Address the checkout experience as a parallel workstream. Creative and landing page optimizations are in progress, but the checkout drop-off is a separate problem that needs a separate solution. Fixing it will compound the impact of everything else.

⭐ Notable Finding What Sets the Top AI Scale Performers Apart

Looking at this period, every top-performing creative in the New AI Scale Engine started as a proven Legacy Statics asset. The AI Scale process took those winning creatives and refreshed the visual treatment - swapping backgrounds, adding design elements like clouds or sparkles, adjusting colors and layout - while keeping the core message intact. That's why they carry strong FluffCo branding: they inherited it from the Legacy originals. The lower-performing creatives in the AI Scale pool were built from scratch without that Legacy foundation, and the results reflect it. It points to a clear takeaway: the AI Scale Engine performs best when it evolves proven Legacy assets, not when it generates new concepts independently.

Key Findings

AI Scale Engine Wins Come From Legacy Roots
Every top-performing AI Scale creative is a visual iteration of a proven Legacy asset. None of the independently generated AI originals are hitting target ROAS. The brief process for new AI assets should start with Legacy winning angles - the creative direction should come from data, not generation alone.
Brief recommendation
Strong Branding = Stronger Performance
All 4 AI Scale Engine top performers feature clear, prominent FluffCo branding in the creative. Lower-performing AI-generated assets lead with discount or price messaging before brand identity is established. Brand visibility in the first frame is a measurable conversion driver - not just a design preference.
Notable insight
Checkout Experience Opportunity
Both engines show a consistent drop-off between Add to Cart and Checkout on get.fluff.co. This is not a creative problem - it's a landing page experience issue. Optimizing the checkout flow would lift revenue across every active campaign without spending more on ads or new assets.
Cross-engine fix
Creative Age is the Primary Drag
The top 3 underperforming Legacy creatives have been running since December–February - up to 97 days. $21,761 in combined spend at 1.66x average ROAS. Implementing a 45-day creative review and retirement process would prevent this accumulation going forward.
Immediate action needed
AI Scale Engine Buyer Quality
Despite similar ROAS, AI Scale Engine buyers spend $11 more per order on average ($157 vs $146). This is a consistent signal that the broader creative testing pool is reaching higher-consideration customers. As AI Scale efficiency improves, this AOV advantage will compound.
Positive signal
get.fluff.co is the Primary Test Surface
All active campaigns are running to get.fluff.co. LP optimizations here benefit every creative at the same time. The advertorial LP represents a future test opportunity - but should be approached with purpose-built new creatives, not by redirecting existing budgets.
LP strategy

Action Plan

🎯

Focus: All actions below are centered on get.fluff.co as the primary conversion surface. The goal for this period is to recover account ROAS above 1.9x by retiring underperforming creatives and scaling proven winners.

Focus AreaStrategic DirectionTypeWhy It Matters
Creative Portfolio Health Several Legacy Statics creatives have been running for 60-90+ days with declining efficiency. The priority is retiring the underperformers and replacing them with assets built on the angles that are proven to work - specifically the hotel comfort and offer positioning that is consistently producing the strongest results across both engines. Refresh Recovers account ROAS back above the 1.9x target by removing the main drag on the portfolio average
AI Scale Engine Brief Quality The clearest finding from this analysis is that AI Scale assets perform well when they start from a Legacy winner and evolve the visual treatment - and underperform when built independently. The next brief cycle for the AI Scale Engine should use the top Legacy performers as the creative foundation, with the AI process focused on visual iteration rather than concept generation. Brief Process Improves the AI Scale Engine win rate and makes the 1,000+ creative volume more efficient at generating returnable spend
Checkout Experience on get.fluff.co Both engines show the same pattern: strong Add to Cart rates followed by a significant drop before checkout completes. This is a site experience issue, not a creative issue, and fixing it benefits every active campaign at the same time. A focused review of the checkout flow on get.fluff.co - friction points, trust signals, payment options - is the highest-leverage non-creative action available right now. Optimize Revenue upside across the full active portfolio without increasing media spend
Advertorial LP as a Growth Test Early data suggests the advertorial landing page format drives meaningfully stronger conversion for the right creative angles. Rather than redirecting existing budgets, the right approach is to build 2-3 purpose-built creatives specifically for this format and run a clean test against get.fluff.co. If the results validate the format, it opens a new scaling path without disrupting what is already working.
